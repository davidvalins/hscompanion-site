H&S Companion

Privacy Policy

Effective 13 July 2026

Overview

H&S Companion is designed to work locally on your device. The core app does not require an account, include advertising or analytics, track you across apps or websites, or sell your personal information. Optional Microsoft 365 features require a Microsoft account and your authorisation.

Data you enter

The app stores the information you choose to enter, which may include profile and workplace details, incidents, risk assessments, inspection records, corrective actions, training and competency records, names, signatures, notes, dates and photographic evidence. You decide what information is appropriate to record.

Storage

App data, settings and inspection templates are stored locally on your device using Apple’s on-device storage technologies. If device backup is enabled, Apple may include app data in a device backup according to your iCloud or computer backup settings.

Camera and photo library

Camera access is optional and is used only when you choose to photograph inspection evidence. You may also select images through Apple’s system photo picker. Selected or captured images are stored with the relevant inspection record; the app does not scan or upload your wider photo library.

Files, sharing and exports

H&S Companion can create PDF reports and export evidence photos. The app does not transmit these files to the developer. Data may leave the app when you initiate an export, select a destination through Apple’s share sheet, or enable an optional connected workflow such as scheduled OneDrive backup, SharePoint synchronisation or a Microsoft email summary. The privacy practices of the destination you choose then apply.

Optional Microsoft 365 connection

If you connect a Microsoft account, authentication is handled by Microsoft. The app stores the refresh token securely in the iOS Keychain and keeps short-lived access tokens in memory. Depending on the features you authorise, the app may use Microsoft Graph to read your basic account identity, upload files to OneDrive, work with selected SharePoint sites, libraries and lists, or send an email through your account. These features are optional, can be disconnected in Settings, and are subject to Microsoft’s and your organisation’s policies.

Data collection and third parties

The app does not include third-party advertising, analytics or tracking services. H&S Companion does not send your safety records or photographic evidence to the developer. When you use an optional Microsoft 365 feature, the data needed for that operation is sent directly to Microsoft services under your authorisation.

Deleting data

You can delete records from within the app. Removing H&S Companion also removes information held in its local app storage, subject to the device’s normal backup and restore behaviour. Files, emails, OneDrive backups or SharePoint items created outside local app storage must be deleted separately from those destinations.

Security and sensitive information

Your device’s passcode, encryption and access controls help protect locally stored data. You can also enable App Lock to require Face ID or the device passcode. Health and safety records can contain confidential or personal information, so you should follow your organisation’s policies when entering, exporting, synchronising and sharing them.

Children’s privacy

H&S Companion is a workplace utility and is not directed to children. The developer does not knowingly collect personal information from children through the app.

Changes

This policy may be updated when the app or its data practices change. The effective date at the top of this page identifies the current version.
