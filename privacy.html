HSCompanion

Privacy Policy

Effective 28 June 2026

Overview

HSCompanion is designed to work on your device. The app does not require an account, include advertising or analytics, track you across apps or websites, or sell your personal information.

Data you enter

The app stores the information you choose to enter, which may include profile and workplace details, risk assessments, inspection records, corrective actions, notes, names, dates and photographic evidence. You decide what information is appropriate to record.

Storage

App data, settings and inspection templates are stored locally on your device using Apple’s on-device storage technologies. If device backup is enabled, Apple may include app data in a device backup according to your iCloud or computer backup settings.

Camera and photo library

Camera access is optional and is used only when you choose to photograph inspection evidence. You may also select images through Apple’s system photo picker. Selected or captured images are stored with the relevant inspection record; the app does not scan or upload your wider photo library.

Files, sharing and exports

HSCompanion can create PDF reports and export evidence photos. The app does not automatically transmit these files to the developer. Data leaves the app only when you initiate an export, choose a folder, or select a destination through Apple’s share sheet. The privacy practices of the destination you choose then apply.

Data collection and third parties

The app does not include third-party advertising, analytics or tracking services. HSCompanion does not send your safety records or photographic evidence to the developer.

Deleting data

You can delete records from within the app. Removing HSCompanion also removes information held in its local app storage, subject to the device’s normal backup and restore behaviour. Files you previously exported must be deleted separately from their chosen destination.

Security and sensitive information

Your device’s passcode, encryption and access controls help protect locally stored data. Health and safety records can contain confidential or personal information, so you should follow your organisation’s policies when entering, exporting and sharing them.

Children’s privacy

HSCompanion is a workplace utility and is not directed to children. The developer does not knowingly collect personal information from children through the app.

Changes

This policy may be updated when the app or its data practices change. The effective date at the top of this page identifies the current version.
