Built for the whole safety workflow

Useful structure,
without the paperwork.

H&S Companion keeps each record, its evidence and the next action connected. It adapts from full-screen iPad to compact Stage Manager windows without losing the work in front of you.

01

Clear risk assessments

Capture hazards and controls, compare initial and residual risk on a 5 × 5 matrix, collect review signatures and create polished PDF reports.

02

Practical inspections

Work through reusable checklists, record findings and add photo evidence directly from the camera or photo library.

03

Actions that stay visible

Keep corrective actions linked to their source, with owners, due dates and status available from one shared view.

04

Incident management

Record what happened, who was affected, immediate controls, investigation details and whether an event may be reportable.

05

Training with signatures

Track toolbox talks, inductions and formal training, manage attendance and capture attendee, trainer or observer signatures.

06

Competency made visible

Define role requirements, connect people to teams and see where competence is current, due or needs attention.

07

Analytics and reminders

Use a live dashboard, KPI trends, search and device notifications to keep overdue work and upcoming reviews visible.

08

Documents and Microsoft 365

Create PDF reports, save locally, or optionally connect Microsoft 365 for OneDrive backup, SharePoint workflows and email summaries.

Local-first and protected

Your records remain
under your control.

Records are stored locally and can be protected with Face ID or your device passcode. External sharing, Microsoft 365 connection, SharePoint sync and scheduled backups are optional features that you configure and control.
