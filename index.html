Built for work on site

Useful structure,
without the paperwork.

HSCompanion keeps the record, the evidence and the next action connected. The detail is there when you need it, and quiet when you do not.

01

Clear risk assessments

Capture hazards, existing controls and further actions, then use a consistent 5 × 5 matrix to make risk easier to understand.

02

Practical inspections

Work through reusable checklists, record findings and add photo evidence directly from the camera or photo library.

03

Actions that stay visible

Keep corrective actions linked to their source, with owners, due dates and status available from one shared view.

04

Reports ready to share

Create readable PDF reports and save or share inspection evidence only when you choose a destination.

Private by design

Your safety records
stay on your device.

HSCompanion stores assessments, inspections, actions, settings and evidence locally. Reports and photos leave the app only when you choose to save or share them.
