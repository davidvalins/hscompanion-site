Help and support

How can we help?

Answers to common questions about records, signatures, notifications, exports and optional Microsoft 365 features.

Email support
Where does HSCompanion store my records?

Incidents, assessments, inspections, actions, training, competence, settings and evidence are held locally by the app on your iPad. A Microsoft account is only needed if you choose to use OneDrive, SharePoint or Microsoft email features.

How is a risk rating calculated?

HSCompanion uses a 5 × 5 matrix. Multiply likelihood by severity to produce a score from 1 to 25. The app groups the result as Low (1–4), Medium (5–9), High (10–15) or Very High (16–25).

How do I add inspection evidence?

Open an inspection finding and choose Take Photo or Choose Photos. Camera access is requested only when you use the camera. Photos remain attached to that inspection unless you delete or export them.

How do I save a PDF report?

Open a risk assessment or inspection and use its PDF options to share the report or save it to Files. The Documents area also brings report and evidence exports together.

How do signatures work?

Risk assessments can collect reviewer approvals, while training records can collect attendee, trainer and observer signatures. Each entry stores the drawn signature with the signatory’s name, role, date and time on that record.

Can I use H&S Companion with Stage Manager?

Yes. The app adapts its navigation and list/detail workspaces as the window changes size. In compact windows, lists move above the selected record and can be collapsed to give the detail more room.

What does the Microsoft 365 connection do?

Microsoft 365 features are optional. When authorised, H&S Companion can back up selected data to OneDrive, connect configured records and reports with SharePoint, and send safety summary emails through your Microsoft account. Your organisation may require administrator approval.

How can I protect the app?

Enable App Lock in Settings to require Face ID or the device passcode when opening the workspace. The normal security, encryption and access controls configured on your iPad also continue to apply.

Can I choose a default export folder?

Yes. In Documents, choose a storage folder. HSCompanion remembers secure access to that folder so future reports and evidence can be saved there more quickly. You can change the folder at any time.

How do I change inspection checklists?

Open Settings, choose Inspections, then select a template. You can edit its name and questions, add a template, adjust scoring thresholds or restore the default templates.

How do I delete app data?

Delete individual records from their workspace. Removing the app deletes its local storage, subject to normal device backup and restore behaviour. Exported files, OneDrive backups and SharePoint records must be deleted separately from their destination.
