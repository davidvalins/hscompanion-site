Help and support

How can we help?

Answers to common questions about assessments, inspections, evidence and exports.

Email support
Where does HSCompanion store my records?

Assessments, inspections, actions, settings and evidence are held locally by the app on your iPad. The app does not require an account or automatically upload records to the developer.

How is a risk rating calculated?

HSCompanion uses a 5 × 5 matrix. Multiply likelihood by severity to produce a score from 1 to 25. The app groups the result as Low (1–4), Medium (5–9), High (10–15) or Very High (16–25).

How do I add inspection evidence?

Open an inspection finding and choose Take Photo or Choose Photos. Camera access is requested only when you use the camera. Photos remain attached to that inspection unless you delete or export them.

How do I save a PDF report?

Open a risk assessment or inspection and use its PDF options to share the report or save it to Files. The Documents area also brings report and evidence exports together.

Can I choose a default export folder?

Yes. In Documents, choose a storage folder. HSCompanion remembers secure access to that folder so future reports and evidence can be saved there more quickly. You can change the folder at any time.

How do I change inspection checklists?

Open Settings, choose Inspections, then select a template. You can edit its name and questions, add a template, adjust scoring thresholds or restore the default templates.

How do I delete app data?

Delete individual assessments, inspections or actions from their workspace. Removing the app deletes its local storage, subject to normal device backup and restore behaviour. Previously exported files need to be deleted from their destination separately.
